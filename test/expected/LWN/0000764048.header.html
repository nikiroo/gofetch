[$] Life behind the tinfoil curtain

([Security] Sep 5, 2018 22:11 UTC (Wed) (jake))

Security and convenience rarely go hand-in-hand, but if your job (or life) requires extraordinary care against potentially targeted attacks, the security side of that tradeoff may win out. If so, running a system like [1]Qubes OS on your desktop or [2]CopperheadOS on your phone might make sense, which is just what Konstantin Ryabitsev, Linux Foundation (LF) director of IT security, has done. He reported on the experience in a [3]talk [YouTube video] entitled "Life Behind the Tinfoil Curtain" at the 2018 [4]Linux Security Summit North America .
