[$] Learning about Go internals at GopherCon

([Front] Sep 5, 2018 19:20 UTC (Wed) (jake))

[1]GopherCon is the major conference for the [2]Go language , attended by 1600 dedicated "gophers", as the members of its community like to call themselves. Held for the last five years in Denver, it attracts programmers, open-source contributors, and technical managers from all over North America and the world. GopherCon's highly-technical program is an intense mix of Go internals and programming tutorials, a few of which we will explore in this article.
